Paper Details

This Political Science question paper is from the 2023 annual examination of Gauhati University for BA 1st Semester students. The paper covers fundamental concepts in political theory and Indian politics.

Examination Type

Annual Examination

University

Gauhati University

Academic Year

2022-2023

Paper Pattern

Theory (70 marks) + Internal (30 marks)

Total Questions

12 Questions (Attempt 10)

Word Limit

Varies by question type

Question Pattern

The question paper follows a structured pattern with different sections:

Section Question Type Marks Number of Questions Instructions
Section A Very Short Answer 2 marks each 10 questions Answer all questions (20 marks)
Section B Short Answer 5 marks each 6 questions Answer any 4 questions (20 marks)
Section C Long Answer 10 marks each 3 questions Answer any 2 questions (20 marks)
Section D Essay Type 15 marks each 2 questions Answer any 1 question (15 marks)

Total Marks: 75 (Theory) + 25 (Internal Assessment) = 100 marks

Syllabus Coverage

This question paper covers the following topics from the BA 1st Semester Political Science syllabus:

Unit 1: Introduction to Political Theory

  • Meaning, nature, and scope of Political Science
  • Approaches to the study of Political Science
  • Basic concepts: State, Sovereignty, Law, Liberty, Equality, Justice
  • Political ideologies: Liberalism, Socialism, Marxism, Fascism

Unit 2: Indian Government and Politics

  • Constitutional development in India
  • Preamble and salient features of Indian Constitution
  • Fundamental Rights and Duties
  • Directive Principles of State Policy
  • Union Executive: President, Prime Minister, Council of Ministers
  • Parliament: Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha

Unit 3: Political Processes in India

  • Party system in India
  • Election process and electoral reforms
  • Federal structure and center-state relations
  • Judicial system and judicial activism
  • Local self-government: Panchayati Raj and Urban Local Bodies

Preparation Tips

To effectively prepare for Political Science examination, consider these strategies:

1. Understand Concepts Thoroughly

Political Science requires conceptual clarity. Focus on understanding:

  • Key political theories and their applications
  • Constitutional provisions and their interpretations
  • Comparative analysis of political systems

2. Practice Answer Writing

Develop the skill of writing precise and well-structured answers:

  • Practice writing within word limits
  • Use headings and subheadings for better presentation
  • Include relevant examples and case studies
  • Practice diagrammatic representation where applicable

3. Use Previous Papers Effectively

This question paper can help you:

  • Identify frequently asked topics
  • Understand the marking scheme and question pattern
  • Practice time management during exams
  • Improve answer presentation skills
