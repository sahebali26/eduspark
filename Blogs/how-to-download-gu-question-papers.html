How to Download GU Question Papers: Complete Step-by-Step Guide

October 15, 2024 5 min read By Saheb Ali

As a Guwahati University student, accessing previous year question papers is crucial for exam preparation. In this guide, we'll show you exactly how to download GU question papers for all semesters and programs.

Step 1: Visit Our Question Papers Section

Navigate to our Question Papers page where we've organized all GU papers by program and semester.

Step 2: Select Your Program

Choose from BA, B.Com, or B.Sc programs. Each program has papers organized by semester (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th, 6th).

Step 3: Choose Your Semester and Subject

Click on your specific semester and subject to access the question paper PDF. We have papers from 2020 to 2024 available.

Step 4: Download or View Directly

All papers are in PDF format. You can either view them directly in your browser or download them for offline study.

Why Use Previous Year Question Papers?

  • Understand exam pattern and marking scheme
  • Identify important topics and questions
  • Practice time management
  • Boost confidence before exams
