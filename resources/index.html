Comprehensive Educational Resources for Students

MiaEdu provides a wide range of educational resources tailored to meet the needs of college and school students. Our collection includes previous year question papers, detailed study notes, solved papers with explanations, and NCERT textbook solutions.

For college students, we offer resources specifically designed for Guwahati University's BA, B.Com, and B.Sc programs. These include semester-wise question papers, subject-specific notes, and solved papers to help with exam preparation.

School students can benefit from our comprehensive NCERT solutions for classes VI to X, covering Mathematics, Science, Social Science, and Languages. We also provide sample papers, revision notes, and additional practice materials for classes XI and XII across Science, Commerce, and Arts streams.
