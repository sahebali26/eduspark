  • Core Concept of Public Administration: Personnel Administration is the management and application of human resources within the public service framework. It is defined as the strategy of selecting new employees and making efficient use of existing ones to obtain the maximum quality and quantity of output and service from the working force. The quality of a nation's administration fundamentally rests upon the skill, foresight, experience, and wisdom of its governing officials.
