Our Story

Founded in 2023, Edu Smartspark began as a small initiative by Saheb Ali and Najma Khatun, two friends with a shared vision to support university students. What started as a YouTube channel sharing question papers has now grown into an educational platform serving students under Guwahati University and across Assam.

Though our journey began locally, we continue to grow steadily, expanding resources and reaching more learners every year.

Who is Saheb Ali? Founder of Edu Smartspark

Saheb Ali is a dynamic educational entrepreneur, tech enthusiast, and community leader from New Piradhara, near Tapattary Block in Abhayapuri, Bongaigaon district, Assam. Born and raised in the serene landscapes of Assam, Saheb Ali has always been driven by a profound passion for education and technology. Currently pursuing a B.A. in Economics (Honours) at Bongaigaon University under the affiliation of Gauhati University, Saheb Ali channels his academic pursuits into real-world impact through Edu Smartspark.

Key Highlights:

  • Founded Edu Smartspark in 2023 to democratize education in Assam
  • Currently pursuing B.A. Economics (Honours) at Bongaigaon University
  • Skilled in web development, graphic design, and video editing
  • Passionate about bridging educational gaps in rural Assam

As the visionary founder of Edu Smartspark (also known as EduSpark), Saheb Ali launched the platform in 2023 with a simple yet powerful mission: to democratize access to quality educational resources for students across Assam. What began as a modest YouTube channel sharing Gauhati University question papers and study notes has evolved into a comprehensive online hub offering free downloads of NCERT books, BA, B.Sc, and B.Com notes, high school resources for classes VII to XII, and innovative learning tools. Saheb Ali's expertise in graphic design using Adobe Photoshop, video editing, and web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript ensures that Edu Smartspark remains user-friendly, visually appealing, and constantly updated.

Educational Impact in Assam

Saheb Ali's commitment extends beyond digital platforms. Residing at his family home in New Piradhara, Assam (PIN: 783384), he actively engages with local communities to bridge educational gaps in rural Assam. Whether it's creating engaging video tutorials on YouTube (@EduSmartspark) or fostering discussions on social media like Instagram (@__saheb_ali) and X (Twitter @sahebali369), Saheb Ali embodies innovation in education. His work has touched thousands of students preparing for Gauhati University exams, HSLC, and AHSEC boards, making him a go-to name for free educational resources in Assam.
