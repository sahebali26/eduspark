Our Story

Founded in 2023, Edu Smartspark began as a small initiative by Saheb Ali and Najma Khatun, two friends with a shared vision to support university students. What started as a YouTube channel sharing question papers has now grown into an educational platform serving students under Guwahati University and across Assam.

Though our journey began locally, we continue to grow steadily, expanding resources and reaching more learners every year.

Our Core Values

Quality Education

We maintain rigorous standards in all our educational materials to ensure accurate, up-to-date content.

Accessibility

Believing education should be free for all, we provide open access to our core resources.

Community

We foster supportive learning communities where students can grow together with free.

Innovation

Continually evolving our Notes & teaching methods to make learning more effective and engaging.
