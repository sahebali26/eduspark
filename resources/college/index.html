Overview

DegreeBachelor of Arts (BA)
Duration4 Years (8 Semesters)
Eligibility10+2 or equivalent
SubjectsMajor, Minor, Elective
UniversityGuwahati University

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) is a four undergraduate program (FYUGP) that provides students with a comprehensive education in humanities, social sciences, and languages. The program is designed to develop critical thinking, analytical skills, and a broad understanding of human society and culture.

At Guwahati University, the BA program follows a semester system with 8th semesters over four years. Students can choose from a variety of subjects including History, Political Science, Economics, English, Assamese, Philosophy, Education, and Sociology.

Program Structure

The BA program is structured to provide both depth and breadth of knowledge:

  • Core Subjects: In-depth study of major subjects
  • Allied Subjects: Complementary subjects that support the major
  • Elective Courses: Optional subjects based on student interest
  • Skill Enhancement Courses: Courses to develop practical skills
  • Project Work: Research projects in the final year

Subjects & Study Materials

The BA program at Guwahati University offers a diverse range of subjects. Below are the major subjects with available study materials:

Competitive Exam Preparation

A BA degree opens up various opportunities for competitive examinations. Here are some popular exams that BA graduates can prepare for:

Popular Competitive Exams for BA Graduates

Civil Services

UPSC conducts Civil Services Examination for IAS, IPS, IFS and other central services.

Banking Exams

IBPS, SBI exams for PO, Clerk and other positions in banking sector.

SSC Exams

SSC CGL, CHSL, MTS for various government department positions.

Teaching Exams

CTET, TET for teaching positions in schools; NET for college lectureship.

Career Options After BA

A BA degree offers diverse career opportunities in both public and private sectors. Here are some popular career paths:

Government Sector

  • Civil Services: IAS, IPS, IFS through UPSC examination
  • State Services: State administrative services through PSC exams
  • Banking: PO, Clerk positions in public sector banks
  • Defense: Army, Navy, Air Force through respective entrance exams
  • Teaching: School teacher through TET, college professor through NET

Private Sector

  • Content Writing: Technical writing, copywriting, content creation
  • Journalism: Reporter, editor, correspondent in media houses
  • Human Resources: HR executive, recruiter, training coordinator
  • Marketing: Marketing executive, brand manager, digital marketer
  • Social Work: NGO positions, community development officer

Higher Education

  • Master's Degree: MA in specialized subjects
  • Professional Courses: Law (LLB), Business (MBA), Social Work (MSW)
  • Research: MPhil, PhD in humanities and social sciences
  • Diploma Courses: Specialized diplomas in journalism, public relations, etc.
